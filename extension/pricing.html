Intelligence / Logic

What is a reasoning call?

A reasoning call occurs when the Mergen Causal Engine analyzes the current runtime buffer to construct a verified causal link and proposed fix for an agent.

Is my data private?

Yes. Mergen is local-first. Signal processing occurs on your machine. We only receive metadata counts for credit allocation, never your logs or code.

How does team sync work?

Nodes communicate over an encrypted peer-to-peer bridge or a private relay, allowing agents to see signals from teammates' execution environments.

Unlimited plans?

We prefer metered honest pricing to ensure the engine remains sustainable for high-volume enterprise workloads while remaining free for individuals.
