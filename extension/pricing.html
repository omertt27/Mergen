Intelligence / Logic

What is a reasoning call?

A reasoning call occurs when the Mergen Causal Engine analyzes the runtime buffer to construct a verified causal link and proposed fix for an agent.

Is my data private?

Yes. Mergen is local-first. Signal processing occurs on your loopback. We only receive metadata counts for credit allocation, never your logs or code.

Source Map resolution?

Paid tiers automatically resolve production stack traces back to original TypeScript/JSX source files locally, providing agents with perfect context.

OTel Compatibility?

Mergen respects and propagates W3C traceparents, allowing it to join browser signals to any OpenTelemetry-instrumented backend service automatically.
